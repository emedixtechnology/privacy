Privacy Policy — Emedix PMJAY Sync

Last updated: 4 July 2026 · Published by Emedix Technology

This browser extension helps registered hospitals synchronise their own patient claim records from the PMJAY National Health Authority (NHA) provider portal, and IPD admission records from Praczin, into the hospital's internal claim tracker. This policy explains what data the extension handles and how.

What data the extension accesses

When you trigger a sync while logged in to the NHA provider portal or Praczin, the extension reads claim and admission records visible in your own authenticated session. This may include patient names, registration or case IDs, admission details, and claim status information belonging to your hospital.

What the extension does with it

The data is transmitted only to your hospital's own claim tracker server, configured by your hospital's administrator. It's used solely to keep your internal tracker in sync with the portals. Nothing runs in the background — data is read only when you click a sync action.

What we do NOT do

We do not sell, rent, or share this data with any third party. We do not use it for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to claim tracking. The extension does not read your browsing history, does not access other websites, and does not collect your login credentials — you log in to the portals yourself.

Storage and security

Synced records are stored on the hospital's own server deployment. The extension itself stores only minimal configuration (such as your tracker server address) locally in your browser. Data in transit is sent over HTTPS.

Data retention and deletion

Retention of synced records is controlled by your hospital's tracker administrator. Removing the extension from your browser deletes its local configuration. To request deletion of records on the tracker, contact your hospital administrator or us at the address below.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised date above.
